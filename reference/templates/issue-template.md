## Labels
- **Type** (required): `type:epic`, `type:story`, `type:task`, `type:bug`, `type:spike`
- **Role** (required): `role:sa`, `role:ba`, `role:pm`, `role:dev`, `role:fe`, `role:ops`, `role:test`, `role:review`
- **Phase**: `phase:requirements`, `phase:architecture`, `phase:development`, `phase:testing`, `phase:review`
- **Priority**: `priority:high`, `priority:medium`, `priority:low`
- **Status**: `status:backlog`, `status:in-progress`, `status:review`, `status:done`
